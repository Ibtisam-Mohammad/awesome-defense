# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to maintain a respectful and constructive community around public defence-adjacent open-source resources.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Keep discussions focused on public physical-defence-adjacent resources and the quality of the list.
- Explain why a resource belongs or does not belong.
- Accept maintainer decisions about scope, quality, and provenance.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language.
- Spam, affiliate promotion, or repeated low-quality submissions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove issues, pull requests, comments, or entries that violate this code or fall outside the repository scope.
